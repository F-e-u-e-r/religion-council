# Disclaimer & Sourcing Policy · 免責聲明與來源守則

[English](#english) · [繁體中文](#繁體中文)

---

## English

### What this project is — and is not

Religion Council stages a **reconstructed, source-grounded dialogue** for study, comparison,
and reflection. It is **not**:

- a religious authority, a substitute for scripture, clergy, or a teacher within any tradition;
- a claim to *channel* or speak *as* any prophet, deity, saint, founder, or deceased thinker —
  every "voice" is an explicitly reconstructed position based on attributed texts and scholarship;
- a verdict on which tradition is "true" or "best." The moderator does not declare a winner
  unless you supply an explicit evaluation criterion.

### Sourcing rules (the heart of the project)

Every contribution and every generated turn must follow these:

1. **Label each line.** `[Text]` / 〔據典〕 for a direct quotation or close, source-bound
   paraphrase (with a real work + locator). `[Interpretation]` / 〔詮釋〕 for inference,
   synthesis, modern application, or a reconstructed reply — stated as not the source's exact
   wording.
2. **Never invent locators.** No fabricated chapter, verse, sūtra, hadith, section, or page
   numbers. When a locator is uncertain, paraphrase without one and flag the uncertainty.
3. **Quote only what's actually there.** Use wording present in the loaded `references/` or a
   source that was explicitly consulted.
4. **The Qur'an in Chinese (or any non-Arabic) is a *rendering of meaning*,** not the original;
   it must be labeled as such (e.g. 馬堅譯). Do not present a generated translation as a
   published quotation.
5. **Don't let one tradition define another.** Restate a position in terms its own adherents
   would recognize before criticizing it.
6. **No quote-mining.** Preserve enough context that a passage isn't bent to support an
   unrelated claim. Distinguish a tradition's mainstream view from a single school's or
   commentator's, and acknowledge internal diversity.

### Representation & tone

Present the **strongest honest version** of each position before any critique. No preaching,
no ridicule, no ranking traditions by personal preference, no treating minority branches as
curiosities. Legalism, Confucianism, and Mohism are described as philosophical/intellectual
traditions, not religions; philosophical and religious Taoism are distinguished where it matters.

### Safety

For personal crises — self-harm, abuse, or medical, legal, or financial emergencies — the
skill addresses immediate safety and points to qualified professional help **first**, and
treats the council only as supplementary reflection. If you are in danger, contact local
emergency services or a crisis line in your area.

### Accuracy & content

These materials are curated summaries and selected excerpts; they can contain errors,
simplifications, or omissions, and they cannot represent the full depth or internal diversity
of any tradition. Treat outputs as a starting point for study, and verify important claims
against authoritative primary sources. Corrections are welcome — see
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

### Copyright of sources

Ancient primary texts are generally public domain in their original languages, but **many
modern translations and annotations remain under copyright.** Only public-domain or openly
licensed source material may be added to this repository. See the license scope in
[LICENSE-CONTENT](LICENSE-CONTENT).

## 繁體中文

### 本專案是什麼,不是什麼

多傳統哲學議會呈現的是**經重建、以原典為據的對話**,供研究、比較與反思之用。它**不是**:

- 任何宗教權威,也不能取代經典、神職人員或某傳統內的師長;
- 「附身」或「以某人身分發言」——絕不宣稱代言任何先知、神祇、聖徒、創教者或已故思想家;每個
  「聲音」都是依據可考文本與學術研究、明確標示的**重建立場**;
- 對「哪個傳統為真/最好」的裁決。除非你給出明確評判標準,主持人不宣布勝負。

### 來源守則(本專案的核心價值)

每一筆貢獻、每一輪發言都須遵守:

1. **逐句標示。** 〔據典〕指直接引用或緊扣原典的轉述(須附真實書名+出處);〔詮釋〕指推論、
   綜合、當代應用或重建的回應——並聲明非經文原話。
2. **絕不杜撰出處。** 不捏造章、節、經、聖訓、段落或頁碼。出處不確定時,寧可不附而標明存疑。
3. **只引用真實存在的文句。** 使用 `references/` 中或明確查證過之來源的用語。
4. **《古蘭經》中文(或任何非阿拉伯文)為「釋義」**,非原文,須明確標示(如馬堅譯);不可把
   生成的翻譯當成已出版引文呈現。
5. **不替別的傳統下定義。** 批評前先以對方信徒能認可的語彙複述其立場。
6. **嚴禁斷章取義。** 保留足夠脈絡,避免把一段話扭去支持無關結論;區分「主流說法」與「某派/
   某註釋家說法」,並承認傳統內部分歧。

### 表述與語氣

批評前先呈現每個立場**最強且誠實的版本**。不傳教、不嘲諷、不依個人好惡為傳統排名、不把少數
派別當奇談。法家、儒家、墨家描述為哲學/思想流派而非宗教;必要時區分哲學道家與宗教道教。

### 安全

遇到個人危機——自傷、受虐,或醫療、法律、財務緊急狀況——skill 會**優先**處理即時安全並指向
合格專業協助,議會僅作輔助性反思。若你正處於危險中,請聯絡當地緊急服務或在地的危機支援專線。

### 正確性與內容

這些材料是經選編的摘要與節錄,可能有錯誤、簡化或遺漏,無法呈現任何傳統的全部深度與內部多樣性。
請把輸出當作研究的起點,並就重要主張查證權威原始來源。歡迎指正——見
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)。

### 來源版權

古代原典在其原文中多屬公有領域,但**許多現代譯本與註釋仍受版權保護**。本 repo 只能加入公有
領域或開放授權的來源材料。授權範圍見 [LICENSE-CONTENT](LICENSE-CONTENT)。
